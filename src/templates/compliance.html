Legal & Compliance

We operate at the intersection of AI innovation and regulatory compliance. Understanding the legal frameworks that protect job seekers.

GDPR Compliance

General Data Protection Regulation

Data Minimization & User Rights

JobGuard AI adheres to Article 5(1)(c) of the GDPR, ensuring only necessary data (job text) is processed.

  • Right to Explanation: We provide confidence scores and "Reasons" for every flag.
  • No Data Retention: Job descriptions are processed in ephemeral memory and discarded.
  • Bias Mitigation: Regular audits to prevent algorithmic discrimination against specific demographics.

FTC Guidelines

Federal Trade Commission

Recruitment & Consumer Protection

We align with the FTC's mission to prevent deceptive business practices.

Truth in Advertising

Ensuring that "Work from Home" claims are verified and substantiated.

Endorsement Guides

Detecting fake reviews or testimonials often used in recruitment scams.

Fraud Act 2006

United Kingdom Legislation

Section 2: Fraud by False Representation

Our system is designed to identify artifacts that constitute "False Representation" under UK Law.

Specifically, we flag postings where the intent is to make a gain for oneself or cause loss to another (the applicant), which is the core definition of fraud under the Act.

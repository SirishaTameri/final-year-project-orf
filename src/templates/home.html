Technical Methodology

01
Data Balancing (Borderline-SMOTE)

We utilize Borderline-SMOTE to address the severe class imbalance in recruitment datasets. By generating synthetic examples near the decision boundary, we improve model sensitivity to fraudulent patterns that mimic legitimate job postings.

02
Transformer Architecture (BERT)

The engine leverages Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers. Our BERT-based approach understands complex semantic relationships, capturing subtle linguistic cues like emotional manipulation and irregular phrasing.
